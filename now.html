Bulletin — From the Lake

last filed · 2026 · V · 12, 09:42 local

Reading

russian short stories

Working slowly through a tattered Penguin anthology — Chekhov in the mornings, Babel after midnight. The point is not to finish; the point is to keep one hand on a second language.

Working on

thesis ch. 3 figures

Last figures, last captions, last passes through Inkscape. The committee meets in late summer; the chapter wants to be done by then. We will see who wins.

Coding

autoencoder, second pass

The first version of the protein autoencoder worked but was ugly; the second tries to be honest about its priors. JAX, Flax, a lot of pen-and-paper before any GPU is permitted to start.

Off the lake

first shift of the season

The boat is back in the water. The first weekend shift is always cold, always windy, always slightly disorganized — and always the best one. Senior deckhand this year; ask me about it.
